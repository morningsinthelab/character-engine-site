FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Character Engine?

Character Engine is the character IP platform inside ConversationOS. We build, manage, and deploy AI characters with memory, personality, backstory, visual identity, and the ability to render live in real time through RunwayML integration — across live shows, social media, websites, and any digital touchpoint.

What can AI characters do for my brand?

AI characters build memory (they remember every interaction), build brand authority (a consistent voice that never goes off-brand), and blur reality (audiences engage with them like real people). They can host live streams with real-time rendering via RunwayML, produce social content, answer questions, onboard users, and represent your brand 24/7.

How does live rendering work?

Character Engine integrates with RunwayML for real-time character animation. Your characters don't just exist as text or static images — they perform live on broadcasts, streams, and video calls. This is the same technology powering the characters on Mornings in the Lab's daily live broadcast.

What's the difference between Standard and Live hosting?

Standard hosting keeps your character available for interactions, content generation, and licensing. Live hosting adds real-time RunwayML rendering — your character can appear live on broadcasts, streams, and video content. Live rendering costs vary based on compute usage.

Can I license existing ConversationOS characters?

Yes. Characters from the MiTL universe (Sterling, Sophia, Preston, Frankie, Tommy, Maya, and 16+ more) are available for licensing. They come with personality, backstory, audience recognition, and live rendering capability. Custom licensing terms start at $2,500/campaign.

Who is behind Character Engine?

Character Engine is built and operated by ConversationOS — the same platform powering Guest Engine, Signal ENGINE, and the Mornings in the Lab daily broadcast network. 1,000+ episodes. 22+ AI characters. Live every day.

What Characters Do for Your Show

Your characters remember past episodes, running jokes, and audience interactions. They reference what happened last Tuesday. They have opinions that evolve. The show develops continuity that keeps listeners coming back.

Your show becomes a universe, not just a podcast. Characters become recognizable voices that audiences follow individually. "Did you hear what Sterling said about that?" That's the conversation you want happening.
